Breath of the Wild Item Search
This app searches for items available in the game,
Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild.

You can search for items by category:
creatures, monsters, equipment, materials, treasure.

Or by location (Greater Hyrule, Faron, Karakiro Village, etc.). You can also select a random category and/or location.

This search will return a random item matching your criteria.
